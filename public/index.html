HOW TO PLAY
  • Goal: survive 8 minutes. Don't let the purple-bordered node (the critical asset) fall, and don't let 7 threats expire.
  • Move: click any connected node.
  • Act: press SPACE at a node with a task or threat for your role.
  • Build (NetEng only): press B, then click any node to build a new link or upgrade an existing one (1 BW).
